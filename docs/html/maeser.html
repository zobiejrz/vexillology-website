Designed by MEnsign.
"Employing the school colors of navy blue and gold, this flag also incorporates the Maeser Lion head. The triangle on the hoist implies that Maeser is always moving forwards and, with the horizontal stripes representing a ladder, upwards. The three stars in the middle stand for the motto “Truth, Honor, Virtue.”"
Designed by Zac Christensen.
"My flag design incorporates a lot of meaning behind what I believe Maeser is. The three stars underneath the Maeser lion head means three things. 1) The number of times Maeser has won #1 school in the state. 2) Truth, Honor and Virtue. 3) Excellence. All three of this ideas is what I believe Maeser represents. The compass represents how Maeser provides a sort of moral compass for our lives, and how Maeser explores different ways of education through the Socratic method and winterm. The gold banner behind represents generosity, strength, and compassion - all components of the environment of Maeser."
